Custom Woods - Vanilla

Adds 22 new types of wood to RimWorld, complete with unique stats, colors, and a massive quality-of-life UI update for your architect menu. 
![Floors](Images/Floors.png)
Features 

 - 22 New Wood Types: Harvest Oak, Maple, Teak, Poplar, and many more directly from their corresponding vanilla trees. (Sorry, Cactus, you're still not a tree).
![List of items](Images/List_of_items.png)
- Unique Stats & Colors: Every wood type has its own color and distinct mechanical stats.
![Comparing weapon stats](Images/Weapon_crafting.png) ![Crafted weapon name](Images/Weapon_name.png)
- Vanilla Integration: Use these new woods to craft weapons, buildings, furniture, floors, bridges, and anything else that normally takes wood.
![Filtered list of items to build](Images/Filtered_list.png)
- Works as Fuel: All new wood types can be burned in campfires, torches, and other fuel-consuming buildings.
- Traders: Bulk goods traders and exotic goods traders will now carry these new wood types, randomly.
- Conversion Recipe: A recipe has been added to convert 2 custom wood into 1 standard Wood log. You will need this for specific vanilla events, such as the Skylantern Festival or anything other mods provide for "Wood only".
![List of bridges](Images/Bridges_list.png)

Quality of Life: UI Filtering 

This mod adds a "flavor" to the game: 

- Wood Filtering: The Architect menu will automatically hide wooden floors, bridges, and items if you don't have the specific type of wood required.
- Stone Filtering: This filtering also applies to most stony floors! The game will no longer tease you with a Marble Floor option when there isn't a single block of marble on the map.
!Note: Items will appear in the menu the moment you have the required materials in your stockpiles or on the map.
![Filtered stone list](Images/Stone_filter.png)

Compatibility 

- Should be compatible with most mods.
- This mod adds new things but does not overwrite core vanilla files (XML patching).
- Recommended: Load this mod after other tree/wood mods if you are using multiple, to ensure the UI filtering works correctly.
	 
!!! Known incompatibility - Biomes! Framework - since this mod creates wooden structures with custom wood, they will be available on the map, but only if Biomes! Framework is not installed. Everything else should work properly.
	 
PLEASE NOTE!
	 These custom woods are not biome-restricted so don't be shocked when you'll see an oaken structure deep in the jungle or cocoa structure on tundra. That's on my ToDo list.
